# Walter — Multi-Strategy Autonomous Trading System

Production-grade autonomous trading with 4 independent strategies + civic accountability scraping.

**Status:** Production-ready | **Agents:** 4 (equity, crypto, biomimicry, publisher) | **Root Cause Fixes:** 4 critical bugs

## 4 Trading Agents

Equity: RSI + VWAP, daily market hours, 8% stop-loss
Crypto: Same strategy, volatility-tuned, 24/7
Biomimicry: Catalyst trading (arXiv/bioRxiv research)
Publisher: Signal aggregation, human approval

## Civic Accountability

6 scrapers: Family court, property records, campaign finance, ethics, contracts, agendas
Pattern detection: Judge bias, GAL concentration, case duration outliers
